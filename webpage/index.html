About Your System

Your HomeAS is able to be self-configured to include devices and sensors logging data around your home. HomeAS enables more complete collection, management, and automated decision-making about your data. HomeAS includes:

  • A centralized, internet-connected, house-specific server that performs most of the computation, collection, and hardware interfacing from other data logging devices,
  • A minimalistic, yet utile, local web interface which allows user viewing and manipulation of data, and control over all peripherals in a local network from a single interface (connected to the central server),
  • As many arduino- and wireless-enabled sensors that send desired data for central processing, and
  • Connectivity to online, cloud, and otherwise web-based services for seamless integration with your online services.
